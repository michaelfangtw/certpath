# Life Topics — AI Conversation Topics

> Source: `docs/life-topics.pdf`
> 16 life-situation topic units for AI conversation practice (FR-9).
> Ordered from most frequent / easiest to most demanding.

---

## Topics (Easy → Difficult)

### 🟢 Beginner

#### 1. 室友日常 — Roommate / Family Chat
*Scenario: After school, cooking dinner together*
- How's your day been? / It's been a long day.
- Who is on trash duty? / Let's just divide up the chores.
- I'll help with the dirty dishes while you make dinner!
- The faucet's been acting up lately. Is it leaking again?
- Adulting is hard. / I feel you. On the bright side…
- Have a blast! / No worries, you do you.

#### 2. 週末怎麼約 — Sharing Life / Inviting & Declining
*Scenario: Friday evening catching up, making weekend plans*
- What is your plan for this weekend?
- We're going on a hike tomorrow. Would you like to come?
- Are you available Friday afternoon? We're checking out a new place!
- Thanks for inviting me but I'm going to have to take a rain check.
- I would love to but I have to get this essay turned in tomorrow.
- I'd love to go, but my schedule won't allow it this time.

#### 3. 茶水間八卦 — Office Small Talk
*Scenario: Coworker small talk in the office bathroom*
- Good to see you! How's your weekend?
- Can you believe how cold / hot it is outside today?
- Did you catch the game last night? I'm not a big baseball fan.
- How's your family / kids? They're doing well — at least from what they told me.
- Any vacation plans coming up for Christmas? I'm saving my vacation days.
- Enjoy it while you can! / See you later!

#### 4. 今天吃什麼 — Ordering at a Restaurant
*Scenario: Ordering at Chipotle and a dessert shop*
- What can I get started for you today? / Can I get a bowl?
- What kind of rice / beans / protein? / Can you do half and half?
- Any salsa? Mild, medium, or hot? / Guac is extra, is that okay?
- Which flavor is the most popular? / Can I get a box of six?
- Cash or card? / Did you need the receipt printed?
- You're all set! Have a good one! / You as well!

#### 5. 買買買 — Shopping & Checkout
*Scenario: Browsing and buying a dress at a clothing store*
- Are you looking for anything specific today? / Just browsing.
- Where is the fitting room? / I'll go try it on.
- Do you have this in a different color? / It comes in these colors.
- Are you looking for a going out dress or a casual one?
- Your total's gonna be $87.63. Cash or card?
- Did you need a bag? / Would you like a receipt?

### 🟡 Intermediate

#### 6. 嗨，你好！ — Meeting New People
*Scenario: New Year's Eve party, meeting strangers*
- Nice meeting you! Where are you from? / What's your major?
- We moved here 10 years ago from Virginia. / I've never been there.
- What brought you back to business school?
- I assume you're gonna practice law? / I don't think that's gonna happen.
- You should pursue it — whatever is true to your heart!
- I'll see you on the rooftop for the countdown later!

#### 7. 陌生人也能聊 — Making Friends / Small Talk
*Scenario: Subway conversation with a stranger in San Francisco*
- Do you know how to use this ticket machine?
- So it's your first time here in SF? / Just in town for a conference.
- What do you do? / What got you into that?
- I got so hooked on this tea — it just went from there.
- I'm gonna meet up with my friends to go rock climbing later. Wanna join?
- You can take my Instagram. / Cool beans!

#### 8. 會說笑就是贏 — Humor & Playful Banter
*Scenario: First date at a restaurant, online dating gone funny*
- You are taller than I imagined. / I'll take that as a compliment!
- I hope I'm not too nerdy. / Does that make you an expert mind reader?
- What's your favorite karaoke song? This is how I judge people.
- I almost canceled tonight — my cat really seemed to need emotional support.
- I just want to put it out there: I am very open-minded.
- I had fun! We'll split the check. / I have to meet my friends in 30 minutes.

#### 9. 陪你說說話 — Emotional Support
*Scenario: Comforting a roommate after a breakup or loss*
- Is everything alright? / I'm sorry to hear that. Take all the time you need.
- What happened? / It's complicated. / We were not on the same page.
- It's for the better. / Sometimes endings are blessings in disguise.
- There are plenty of fish in the sea. Get out and mingle!
- The silver lining is that it gives you time to focus on yourself.
- My condolences. / She'll come out of it. Is there anything I can do for you?

#### 10. 房子壞了怎麼辦 — Home Maintenance
*Scenario: AC breaks down, calling a repair service*
- I'm calling because my AC is not working. It hasn't turned on since yesterday.
- I was wondering when you guys can come out and check it.
- The compressor blew out and you're also low on refrigerant.
- The total before taxes is gonna be $5,325.25. Holy moly!
- I would recommend possibly looking at replacing your unit — in the long run.
- I also noticed the thermostat seems off. / Thanks for stopping by!

#### 11. 投訴有門 — Customer Complaints
*Scenario: Rental car flat tire on the freeway, calling customer service*
- I'm calling because the tire pressure light has been on.
- I apologize for the inconvenience. Where are you located?
- We will send someone to tow your car away. The tow truck is on the way.
- Our flight is taking off in 4 hours. That wouldn't work — we were cutting it close.
- Will the cost be compensated? / What would you suggest?
- I might have to miss my flight. / You can talk to the branch manager.

#### 12. 出發去旅行 — Travel & Accommodation
*Scenario: LAX airport immigration + hotel check-in*
- What are you here for? / I'm here for a vacation. / How long do you plan to stay?
- What is your profession? / I am a marketing manager for a tech company.
- Can I see the credit card that you used for your reservation?
- I was wondering if you have any suite upgrades available today?
- I upgraded you to an ocean view suite on the 33rd floor with a Jacuzzi.
- Did you need one or two key cards? / There is a $20 per day resort fee.

### 🔴 Advanced

#### 13. 我不舒服 — Health & Medical
*Scenario: Clinic visit with fever and diarrhea*
- What brings you here today? / I've been having a fever since yesterday, diarrhea too.
- Fill out this top portion of the form, sign, and put down the date.
- Any exposure to someone with a flu? / Are you allergic to any medications?
- Your heart rate is a bit elevated — not so much that we need to worry about.
- Chills and headaches are primarily caused by dehydration.
- Try to stay away from spicy food, greasy food, and dairy. / Feel better!

#### 14. 面試不緊張 — Work & Job Interviews
*Scenario: Marketing representative job interview*
- Can you briefly tell me more about yourself? / Walk me through your previous experience.
- My main responsibilities include monitoring paid ad campaigns and reaching out to new distributors.
- Can you share an example of a marketing campaign you worked on?
- What made you want to look for a new job? / I felt stagnant. I want to bring value to a new company.
- What are your salary expectations? / I'm open to discussion — I have 2 other offers on the table.
- Our benefits include medical, dental, eye care, 401k, and life insurance. / We will be in touch.

#### 15. 聊聊最近怎麼了 — Discussing Current Events
*Scenario: Watching TV news and discussing it with a roommate*
- Just a little down in the dumps. / Prices have been skyrocketing.
- I was looking at the polls. / It will have a negative impact on the economy.
- Most of it's not even objective news reporting — it's just sensationalism.
- But one thing led to another. / Look at the housing market.
- You are only bringing yourself down. / Tell you what…
- It is already behind me. / Caught red-handed!

#### 16. 說說你的想法 — Sharing Values & Perspectives
*Scenario: Discussing racial stereotypes and political correctness*
- What has brought this on? / I am feeling gloomy. / I just lay back and forget everything.
- This program contains disrespectful depictions of culture.
- We want the audience to recognize the negative impact of these opinions.
- Isn't that a little bit over the top? / I think children are too young to understand such concepts.
- The very term "being politically correct" started in America. / I didn't know that was a thing.
- The world is hiding its head in the sand. / Where is the fun in that?

## Difficulty Rationale

| Level | Why |
|-------|-----|
| 🟢 Beginner | High-frequency, predictable scripts; short turns; familiar daily vocabulary |
| 🟡 Intermediate | Longer exchanges, spontaneous social language, service complaints, emotional nuance |
| 🔴 Advanced | Domain-specific vocabulary (medical, legal, HR), opinion expression, abstract concepts |
